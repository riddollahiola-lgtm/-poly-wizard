About

Six years across product and character pipelines.

I split my time between hard-surface product design and stylized character work — two disciplines that share more than they seem to. Both come down to reading a silhouette, respecting how a surface will actually be made or moved, and knowing when a render is telling the truth about the model underneath it.

Before freelancing, I worked in-house on consumer electronics and mobile game art. I still keep both practices active — it's the fastest way I know to stay honest about topology, since a mesh that has to deform can't hide behind a good material.
